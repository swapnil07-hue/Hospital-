About Our Hospital

We are the Nelson hospital (best Multispeciality hospital in Nagpur) grown over the last 15 years from a dedicated Tertiary Child Care Hospital (Nelson Hospital formerly known as central India’s Child Hospital) to a big NABH and ISO Accredited Multispeciality Hospital catering to multidisciplinary Care across central India. 

Nelson Hospital  Best Multispeciality Hospital in Nagpur has got one of the best infrastructures in central India having NABH  accreditation and ISO 9001:2015 as feathers in its cap.

 We have all super specialties including Pediatric and Neonatology, Neurology and Neurosurgery, Gynaecology and Obstetrics,  Cardiology and Cardiac Surgery, Day Care Surgery, Orthopedic and Joint Replacement, Gastroenterology, Internal Medicine & Critical Care, Oncology and Oncosurgery, Nephrology with Dialysis and Trauma Centre to provide the best comprehensive treatment under one roof. Nelson Hospital has been working for improving the lives of a low socio-economic group of society in association with ABMSS and RBSK. We have done a number of cleft palate and lip surgeries, Cardiology, and Cardiac Surgery, cochlear implant surgeries, and pediatric cardiac surgeries.
